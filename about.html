About Me

Hi, I'm Alice! I am currently a UX/UI design student at RMIT passionate about creating intuitive and aesthetically pleasing designs. With a background in the arts, graphic design and a keen interest in user experience, I strive to build products that delight users and solve real-world problems.

My journey into design started with a love for art and technology. Over the years, I have honed my skills in various design tools and methodologies, allowing me to create effective and engaging user experiences.
